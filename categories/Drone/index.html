Quadcopter Drone, Part 2 : Mathematical Modeling

After showing the functional principle of the quadcopter (direction control, motion types, ..) in the part I of Quadcopter Drone series, we want to model mathematically the quadcopter from scratch in this part. We will develop the related differential equations of the the quadcopter motions by using the Euler-Lagrange principle.
